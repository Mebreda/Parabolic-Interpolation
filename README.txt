1. Run interface.py
2. Enter your equation on the first box
      (Note: always put "*" on multiplication. Ex. 4*x^2 + 12*x^3 or 2*(x^2)
3. Enter the three initial guesses on the next three boxes
4. Click "Solve" to solve the program or "Clear" to remove the inputs
5. Click "Show Solution" at the lower right of the window to show the process
6. Click "Clear" to enter a new problem
